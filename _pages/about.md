## About Me

I am currently serving as a **Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the European University of Bangladesh**. I am also pursuing an **M.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering at BRAC University**.

I completed my **B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering from BRAC University**, where my undergraduate thesis was titled **“ML Based Career Suggestive System for Informal Job Sector Considering Cognitive Skills.”**

My academic journey combines teaching, software development, and research in artificial intelligence. I am particularly interested in building AI systems that are not only accurate but also explainable, trustworthy, and useful in practical environments.

## Research and Publications

My research work covers several areas of artificial intelligence and applied computing, including medical imaging, disease classification, phishing detection, time-series forecasting, EEG-based disease detection, multimodal AI, and trustworthy AI.

Selected research topics include:

- Breast cancer histopathology analysis
- Gallbladder disease classification using Swin Transformer
- Tea leaf disease classification and out-of-distribution detection
- Parkinson’s disease gait forecasting using VAR, LSTM, and GRU
- EEG-based epilepsy detection
- Phishing detection using VLM and LLM reasoning
- Byzantine fault tolerance in network intrusion detection
- AI-based automated peer-review protection

A detailed list of my journal articles, preprints, conference papers, and ongoing submissions is available on the [Publications page](/publications/).
